Music

    There are two sides to music. I prefer to listen to soft music when I am working, thinking, or when I want to take a break. Such music could be of environmental music such as The Riverlands, the album Exit Plan, or Library Stroll where I am able to let myself flow though my mind. Genres of easy listening music like bossa nova, lounge, and city pop are also part of my playlist; they are of what I feel personally as music for traveling. Examples include Give up on it, starlight, and Magic Way. I also give special attention to folk and chiptune music as a niche genre that is diverse in their works. The works of Ringing Volcano never fail to impress me with how cute, yet evocative, their instruments play. FearofDark always is a solid artist when it comes to chiptune.
